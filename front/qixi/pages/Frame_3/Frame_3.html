A romantic story
The legend of the Tanabata Festival is the love story of the Cowherd and the weaver girl. The Cowherd and the weaver girl are men and women, who love each other and live a very happy life. But later, it was known by the queen mother, who separated the Cowherd and the weaver girl. Only on the seventh day of July in the lunar calendar every year, can the two meet on the magpie bridge
